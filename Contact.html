Academic Fee Structure (Per Year)

The standard annual course fee for both Class 11 and Class 12 is ₹8,400. Students can choose from the following payment options:

• Installment Option: Pay in two installments of ₹4,500 each, totaling ₹9,000 (slightly higher than the standard fee to cover processing and flexibility benefits).
